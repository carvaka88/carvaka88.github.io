Aryans and British India, Thomas R. Trautmann, ISBN 0-520-20546-4, Call number DS425.T68 1997, University of California Press. Trautmann came across a stone inscription in Sanskrit near the entryway of the Old Indian Institute Building at Oxford. From viewing this, the special character of British concept of Aryan came to him as an epiphany.
