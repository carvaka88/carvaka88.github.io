Hindi and Urdu

title: “Hindi and Urdu” authors: author: “Carvaka88” email: “carvaka88@gmail.com” Hindi and Urdu (Hindustani/Hindvi) have the same syntax and grammar and the language shares its origin with other Indo-European languages such as Bengali, Oriya, Gujarati, Bengali, Marathi etc. Urdu nouns and adjectives have origins in Arabic, Persian, Turkish, Pushtu etc but over ninety-nine percent of verbs have their roots in Sanskrit or Prakrit (http://www.dawn.com/news/681263/urdus-origin-its-not-a-camp-language) Urdu as a name for the language seems to have occured for the first time in 1780s.
